Did you know? Earth is the third planet from the Sun in our solar system. It's the only known celestial body to support life as we know it. Approximately 71% of its surface is covered in water, earning it the nickname "the Blue Planet." Fun Facts: Earth's diameter is about 12,742 kilometers (7,918 miles). It takes about 365.25 days for Earth to orbit the Sun, giving us our calendar year. Our planet is home to a diverse range of ecosystems and over 7 billion humans.
